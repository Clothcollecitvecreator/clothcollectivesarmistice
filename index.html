AUTHOR'S NOTE
Misfire was never part of a plan. It came together to help me express something I couldn’t articulate at the time. Consider it more like a mixtape — made in the middle of it, not after it was figured out. The heart design sits in that space — not broken, not whole, just honest. It reflects what it feels like when a connection shifts and you’re left adjusting to the quiet that follows. The imagery explains what I couldn’t at the time. Built in one month, the process moved the same way: long nights, restarts, reflection, slowly making sense of it. Misfire ends with this design, living through those who wear it as a documented moment and a step toward something steadier. The search for Armistice continues.
