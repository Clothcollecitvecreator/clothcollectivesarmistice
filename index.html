AUTHOR'S NOTE

This project exists somewhere between documentation and memory. Misfire began as a moment, then became an object, and now lives as a record of something that could not be explained directly. Every image, every frame, every decision is part of an archive rather than a campaign. This is not just a garment but a filed entry in a larger body of work, preserved in its original condition and released without revision.
